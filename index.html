Efrain Tinoco

Hello everyone! My name is Efrain Tinoco. Professionally, I have over 20 years of experience with Ford Motor Credit. I currently supervise a diverse team of 12 CSR’s, which I help train and motivate towards individual and group goals. I ensure daily tasks and functions are completed accurately, timely, and per Company Policy and Procedure. I am a self-starter, and detailed oriented, with great written and verbal communications skills.

Personally, I am married to a beautiful wife and father of two wonderful teenagers. I'm the biggest Dallas Cowboys fan you will ever meet. I was born and raised in Dallas, TX., where I still live, and I am of Mexican-American descent. I am very proud of my culture and think it is important to make sure my kids understand and embrace their heritage. As such, I make it a point to celebrate Day of the Dead every year. So my kids know the importantance of remembering and honoring our loved ones who have passed away.
